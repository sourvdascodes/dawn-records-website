Types of Glasses


  1. Wired Glass
    • Bent Glass
      -Glass for round edges or stairs
    • Heat Glass
      -Glass primarily used for Wind Shields and headlights
    • Panel Glass
      -Mainly used at skyscraper walls
  2. Float Glass
    • Reflective Glass
      Primarily choice for normal household Glass
    • Frosted
      Used as Offices, Cabin, Washrooms and Bathrooms
    • Commercial Glass
      For goverment and non-goverment project
  3. Figure Glass
    • Textured Glass
      Used as semi-transparent medium
    • Stripes
      Styling for Office works
    • Chess
      Same as Office, but also for Banks
  4. Plain Glass
    • Transpatent
      Used as everyday glass(probably most sold glass so far)
    • Toughned Glass
      For doors and walls
    • Mirrors
      Used at Everywhere
